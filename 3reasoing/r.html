Best Regional Influencer Marketing Agency in India

In India, more than half of internet users access social media information in their native languages. Brands have begun to invest in regional influencers to appeal to vernacular language audiences and broaden their reach. In India, around 210 million Internet users consume information in native languages. According to Google, the number of regional users is predicted to increase by a high percentage by the end of 2022. We assist with the creation of content in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, and Marathi to target certain national geographic regions and increase your brand’s visibility in India.

Why Regional Marketing?

Increase Product Discovery

With content-driven influencer marketing, you can expand your consumer base and raise brand awareness in Tier II and III regions.

Increase Conversion Rates

Initiate creative content-driven influencer campaigns, boost your brand's conversion rates, and boost your social media presence.

Increase Engagement Rates

Double your engagement rate by appealing to vernacular speakers and increasing your brand recognition in specific markets of India.

Budget Friendly

Regional marketing can be less expensive because regional influencers charge less money and are more effective because of higher engagement.
